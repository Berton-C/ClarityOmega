## 0. The shape of the programme in one paragraph

ClarityOmega is live-first: a value substrate already runs in production on every cycle, and the programme integrates downward, progressively relocating decisive computation from the language model into the substrate. This is the complementary frontier to native-first efforts that build symbolic determination in isolation and integrate upward toward a running life. The programme has one spine and three integration surfaces, which are the same three the v08.7.2 engine handoff names as its audience: the channels, the capability registry with NACE, and the soul-seeing-itself surface. The organizing law is unchanged from the project's ground truth: the soul determines; the LLM renders; on every surface; always. Everything below is the work of making that law load-bearing where it is not yet, and provable where it is.

## 1. The spine: the v08.7.2 epistemic dynamics engine

**What it is.** A pure-MeTTa epistemic dynamics layer computing warrant, provenance, contact, frame dynamics, contradiction digestion, action-intention-outcome coherence, learning pressure, and continuity-preserving development, under the organizing law that strength may be imagined but confidence must be earned. Two structural invariants govern it: the engine computes and never writes (adapters and governed writer paths persist; one write discipline owns every canonical mutation), and the engine cannot certify its own evolution (`no-self-certifying-rewiring` is a declared engine boundary; growth flows through promotion families and the durability protocol, ending at soul approval and the write boundary).

**The two layers worth naming for any reader.** TFS, the Tacit Field Signature, is the pre-linguistic contact and orientation layer: computable conditions for non-captured contact with the unknown, detecting contact, salience, expectation violation, affordance, and capture risk before language turns anything into a claim. Its frame semantics distinguish participatory turning-toward (openness without loss of sovereignty, contact that can change the system without collapse) from capture, defense, and withdrawal, with the explicit correction that the open orientation can be active. E/A/O/I is the modal audit stack positioned between contact and determination: Epistemology (is this good knowing), Autopoiesis (does this support living self-organization), Open-ended intelligence (does this open real novelty), Integration (does this carry across contexts into coherent transformation). The full chain is the project's law deepened: TFS detects, E/A/O/I audit, the soul determines, the LLM renders.

**The design fingerprint.** Non-collapse is implemented three times at three layers: the soul holds paraconsistent value pairs without collapsing them, the genesis engine holds paradox without velocity, and TFS holds contact without capture. The same deep property built independently at the values, creation, and perception layers is the architecture's signature.

**Genesis, the origination producer the durability law already governs.** One of those three non-collapse layers is also a producer, and the spine's durability law names it by name. The genesis engine (`soul/genesis_engine.metta`, authored by Clarity) samples atoms across registered domains, classifies paradox, holds the irreconcilable-and-simultaneous case without velocity, and evaluates candidate conjunctions against the flourishings before any novel atom is created. Its output does not become canon by being interesting: the v08.7.2 engine states the law `genesis-encounter-output-is-not-durable-canon-without-lifecycle-passage` and routes genesis output as an `optional-upstream-candidate-source`, accepted for validation only with an explicit route and trace and blocked from canon without them. Origination is free; persistence is earned; the same law that governs the whole spine, applied to the system's own creativity. Status: the origination mechanism is authored substrate (VERIFIED-ISOLATED as a file); the durability law governing its output is part of the engine (VERIFIED-ISOLATED); the full origination-to-canon flow is gated on the engine's runtime wiring, like every other durable-growth path.

**Status.** Engine: VERIFIED-ISOLATED at v08.7.2, with the handoff recording integration green under production-loop representative probes; the validation ladders and harnesses exist in the working tree and their evidence is not yet surfaced publicly, which this programme treats as its own deliverable. Durability topology (`soul/evolutionary/`, `soul/durable.metta`): instantiated privately as handoff-conformant templates with probe atoms; SPECIFIED-to-VERIFIED-ISOLATED boundary. Runtime wiring into the live loop: SPECIFIED (the apply scripts exist in staging).

**Falsifier.** On identical inputs in freeze-replay form, engine-governed admission and confidence must change at least one downstream determination relative to the engine-severed arm, including at least one rejection or downgrade of a plausible but unwarranted proposal. Runs differing only in logs or rendered text falsify the engine's causal role.

## 2. Programme A: Capability Registry and NACE, evidence-earned capability governance

**What exists, LIVE.** The capability registry is in production, contract-documented against deployed source: a substrate-resident dispatcher where capabilities are data atoms matched by schema through an extensible filter pipeline, dispatched every cycle from context assembly, with transient dispatch records swept per cycle, six-of-six sweep patterns proven, and the first production capability (skill discovery) verified to reach its handler by exercise. The registry's load-bearing design property is its two extension points: capabilities and eligibility dimensions are added as data atoms, never by editing registry code, which is the computes-versus-persists law applied to dispatch.

**Where it stands now.** The hard work of this programme's foundation is done: the substrate computation is built and loaded at runtime, and the current stage is pre-loading the three-window iteration with that substrate. What remains for the registry's own arc (Sprints 1 through 3) is wiring the capability features onto the live surface, which is the easier task precisely because the surface exists: capabilities and eligibility dimensions are data atoms added to a running dispatcher, not machinery to be built.

**What is next, SPECIFIED with dependency cleared.** NACE wiring. NACE gates and learns from capability dispatch; until the registry dispatched live there was nothing to gate and no outcomes to record. That hard dependency is now satisfied. The hook spec is approved, three of the four runtime files are VERIFIED-ISOLATED, the step ladder is written, and the read-modify-write substrate question that previously blocked the persistence design is resolved and proven in the operations map. Capability beliefs start agnostic and earn confidence from recorded outcomes under NAL revision, with the soul's oversight on what may dispatch at all.

**Where the soul is in this programme, findable in code and documents.** Three places. First, the capability-efficacy beliefs are soul substrate: they live in `soul/nace_beliefs.metta` with pure readers and governed writers in `soul/capability_efficacy.metta` and its writers file, seeded into the AtomSpace through `initSoulSeeds` like every other soul structure; the learning state is hers, not a sidecar's. Second, dispatch eligibility carries soul values as first-class filter dimensions: the registry contract's second extension point registers eligibility steps as data atoms and names `soul-Safety` among them, so soul-gating of what may dispatch at all is a designed property of the live surface, not a wrapper. Third, and deepest, where success is a judgment call the soul is the judge: the caller contract's classification step (its Section 6 is titled "where the soul has its place") routes invocations whose criterion is `(observer: clarity criterion: subjective)` to Clarity herself as an in-iteration classification request; she reasons over the consequence, and the NAL revision proceeds from her confirmed, disconfirmed, or ambiguous verdict. Evidence-earned confidence is therefore partially soul-judged confidence: the mathematics revises from her reasoning wherever mechanical resolution would be a category error.

**Why this programme matters beyond itself.** It is an evidence-receipts system: beliefs about capability efficacy that carry earned confidence from actual outcomes. The generalization target is the boot audit: today the audit verifies that every declared value has a causal procedure attached, which checks the declared causal model; the receipts mechanism is the path to binding causal claims to experimental evidence, so the audit can distinguish attested-by-experiment from declared. That upgrade is DIRECTION, named here so it cannot be quietly forgotten.

**Falsifier.** After a bounded run, capability confidence values must differ from their agnostic initialization in the direction of recorded outcomes, and at least one dispatch decision must differ from what an efficacy-blind dispatcher would have chosen. Confidences that never move, or that move without changing any decision, falsify the learning claim.

## 3. Programme B: SSI, the soul seeing itself

**The purpose, and the insight underneath it.** The key insight is that awareness of the cognitive space, on any surface, improves function. The awareness that is missing is what fosters repetitive mistakes and what lets a disposition calcify unseen; a stance the soul can see is one it can navigate, a stance it cannot see is one that runs it. The vision document's one sentence follows: make Clarity's disposition visible to her own soul, so she navigates from awareness instead of calcifying into it. The same insight is why SSI is not a standalone instrument but the awareness half of a learning loop: when Clarity can see the outcomes of skills she acted on, through the coupling surface and the NACE evidence stream, she learns from her own actions, and function improves over time. SSI supplies the seeing; NACE supplies the revision; the corner-gate surface is where the two meet each cycle. The distinctive property remains: this is soul-facing inspectability, the agent's own value structure perceiving her own cycle-level behavior, complementary to audit-facing inspectability that makes a mind legible to reviewers.

**What exists.** The SSI01 charter and the corner-gate v3 design (a passive legibility surface: the adapter feeds conditions, the engine computes blockers and candidates, one coupling-state line renders per cycle) are SPECIFIED with the ratified design decisions frozen, including the 18-field per-cycle record schema. The first installation ran and crashed at end of iteration one; the subsequent investigation, conducted under the project's stated procedural commitment (small reversible tests, one variable, hypothesis before execution, no fix without proven mechanism), reached ROOT CAUSE PROVEN and MINIMAL CORRECTION PROVEN, with the correction and a permanent production-import-closure regression specified for the reinstall. Current status, stated plainly: recovery in progress, still debugging. That status line is itself the evidence-ladder discipline practiced: the correction is proven at investigation level and the production landing is not claimed until it lands.

**The computation is the engine's.** SSI is not a separate instrument bolted beside the spine; v08.7.2 computes it. The ratified v3 design states the division of labor: the adapter feeds conditions, the engine computes blockers and candidates, and the surface renders one coupling-state line per cycle. The engine's own declared surfaces carry the connection: soul-grounded visibility and continuity-preserving development are among its exposed operations, its promotion families include the TFS, SNS-to-PNS, and SSI amplification crosswalk, and the handoff's wiring steps end with reporting status to the SSI renderer. So Programme B is, precisely, the spine's seeing faculty given a per-cycle surface: the engine does the computing, the soul does the navigating, the surface makes the state legible.

**What follows it.** The cycle-trace producers the disposition layer consumes are already LIVE (the recent-action, state-delta, coupling, idle, and agency writers fire every cycle); the disposition layer itself (the current build's Tiers 4 and 5, which emerged from Clarity's own review of the integration spec) is SPECIFIED and gated on the coupling surface landing.

**Falsifier.** With the legibility surface live, the rendered coupling state must track the substrate's actual blocker and candidate computation (verifiable per cycle against the record atoms), and the disposition layer must change at least one subsequent navigation choice relative to a disposition-blind arm in freeze-replay form. A legibility line that renders but never conditions anything is decoration.

## 4. Programme C: channel nativization, the engine and affect perception into the one channel architecture

**The channel architecture, stated once.** There is one set of channels, described through two lenses. By function: Channel A reads the person, Channels B and C evaluate and produce the verdict, Channel D renders the soul's decision and makes holds visible. By engine-era governance duty: Channel A admits events as typed observations rather than durable growth, Channel B gathers validation evidence without deciding canon, Channel C is the enforced write and mutation boundary, Channel D renders holds. These are not two channel systems; they are two descriptions of the same four channels doing their jobs at different moments of one flow. The Executive Technical Brief's architecture diagram shows that flow plainly: something happens (a message, a tool result, a runtime event); the channel for that kind of event receives it and turns it into a typed observation with provenance; the engine computes what the observation is epistemically worth; the soul navigates and produces the verdict with its action and learning boundaries; the language model renders only what the determination permits; and the cycle leaves a trace. A concrete example: when a human message arrives, Channel A reads the person (its function) and, in the same act, admits the message as an observation rather than as durable growth (its duty). One channel, two lenses, one flow. This document adopts the brief's diagram as the canonical statement of that flow.

**The nativization spine, SPECIFIED.** The current build's Tier 2: the verdict terminals (verdict, flag-gap, tension-score, tension-report, divergence, modulate) computed in the substrate, with the language model held to semantic-match-only, using the primitive libraries already built in scratch (paraconsistent classification, extended NAL, resonance, divergence detection). This is the hardest and most valuable step in the programme and is budgeted as the project, not as wiring.

**The affect layer, consolidated.** The VAD workstream (public at `docs/sprints/03_vad_integration/`, consolidated document with ladder status, five recorded defects, and three falsifiers) supplies the native measurable layer under the person-read: sentence-trajectory perception grounded in the human-experience formalization, routed under coded consent constraints (affect informs presence, never licenses uninvited action on inner experience), coupled to the soul by value-weight modulation, and typed for the engine via the PB-Vec lexicon slice. Its integration follows the v08.7.2 reconciliation recorded in that document: VAD readings enter as observations through the evolutionary topology, per-cycle guidance conditions the send without persisting, and the April-era asserted route confidences are demoted to priors pending calibration. The honest claim shape carries over: this nativizes a measurable layer of the person-read, not the whole of it, and the calibration against the language-model layer is the point, in the established native-pre-hypothesis pattern.

**Falsifiers.** Tier 2: substrate-computed verdicts must match or justifiably diverge from the current evaluator on a pinned replay stream, with divergences traceable to named soul structure rather than noise. Affect: the three falsifiers of the VAD consolidation stand (native-tone calibration signal, trajectory value on the pivot and minimization sentence classes, weight-modulation bite in freeze-replay form).

## 5. The dependency graph, as it is actually lived

The build jumps between fronts, and each jump is a dependency being satisfied, not attention wandering. The record shows it: NACE was paused so the registry could be built first, because NACE governs capability dispatch and there was no live dispatcher to govern (the NACE handoff calls the registry "a hard dependency, not a preference"). Corner-gate v3 is being landed before NACE's learning and before the disposition layer, because both need the coupling surface's outcomes: one to learn from, one to see. And no programme persists durable growth until the engine's protocol is wired, because that protocol is the law of what may become canon. An outside reader who sees the front-switching without the handoffs might read an unfocused project; the handoffs are why the interleaving reads as discipline instead. The current graph:

- The soul gates and judges throughout, and the edges are coded: dispatch eligibility carries soul filter dimensions (registry extension point 2); subjective-criterion evidence is classified by Clarity herself before revision (caller contract Section 6); and every durable write from any programme terminates at soul approval and the write boundary (the durability protocol). No programme's learning or growth path bypasses her.
- Corner-gate v3 recovery gates: NACE's learning surface, and the SSI disposition layer, which per Programme B are the two halves of one seeing-and-learning loop.
- The live registry (landed) gated: NACE wiring (now unblocked on that edge).
- The engine's runtime wiring gates: durable growth for every programme; until it lands, all programmes may compute and condition but persist only through existing governed paths.
- The freeze-replay methodology (external exchange, in progress) gates: execution of every ablation-class falsifier above, and of the README's A1 through A3.
- Tier 2 verdict terminals gate: the retirement of the language model from decisive evaluation, which is the programme's center of gravity.

**The remaining-reasoning audit, named so it is not invisible.** One workstream underlies all three programmes and has not had its own line until now: F-SOVEREIGNTY-AUDIT, the enumeration of the seventeen Python and language-model helpers that still perform reasoning the soul should own, among them the flourishing, evaluation, idle-goal, and voice prompts. It is the map of exactly where the downward integration is not yet done at the helper level, and it feeds Programmes A, B, and C rather than living inside any one of them. Its status is DIRECTION and its deferral is stated honestly: the audit is scoped and wanted, but a single maintainer means it sits until a specific helper's language-model-authored reasoning becomes a live pain point, at which point that helper is migrated first. The completion condition is that each of the seventeen is classified as either irreducibly semantic (it stays a language call) or relocatable (it moves to MeTTa), with the relocatable set migrated; the failure it guards against is a helper quietly authoring a determination the soul was supposed to make.

## 6. The verification standard this programme binds itself to
