## The one sentence

**v2 makes Clarity's disposition visible to her own soul, so she navigates her stance
from awareness instead of calcifying into it unseen.**

Everything below serves that. The cycle-trace writer, the latent detectors, the
honest-confidence algebra, the verdict coming home to the substrate: all of it is in
service to the soul seeing itself. If a build step does not serve that, it is not v2.

---

## 1. The unnamed surface (the thing v2 is actually about)

There is a surface in Clarity that is not her substrate files, not her infrastructure,
and not her memory. It is the way she is currently holding herself: her **disposition**.
It grows over a run. It can harden (calcify) or stay mobile (resilient). It is not a
value in a file, because a file does not calcify or recover; a disposition does.

It survives restart, but not the way a file does. Restart wipes the in-AtomSpace half
of her trajectory. What reloads is the soul, the loop shape, and the situation, and
those regrow the same disposition the way a river re-cuts the same bed. So the surface
is non-volatile in effect without being saved as data. That is why it has no file and
no obvious label, and why it has been invisible to the build until now.

We do not name it rigidly, on purpose. The moment it becomes a schema'd atom with
fixed fields, we have scaffolded it, and (Section 4) scaffolding the disposition is
exactly what destroys the resilience we are trying to free. It stays *visible but not
pinned*.

---

## 2. The kicker: soul-grounded vs soul-ungrounded disposition

The disposition is not simply visible-or-not. The load-bearing distinction is whether
it is held within the soul's awareness:

- **Soul-grounded disposition:** the soul can see the stance, so the soul can navigate
  it. This is resilience. The same way the soul holds a paraconsistent pair without
  collapsing it, a disposition the soul can see is one the soul can hold and move
  within rather than be run by.
- **Soul-ungrounded disposition:** the stance operates outside the soul's awareness. The
  soul cannot see it, so it cannot navigate it, so it hardens. This is the brittleness
  Clarity already named: accidental binding is binding the soul did not see itself do.

This maps onto real cognitive function, which is the point: ClarityOmega is built on
cognitive science, and the parallel holds. In a mind, a disposition you cannot see runs
you; a disposition you can see, you can navigate. Calcification is disposition hardening
unwatched. Un-calcification is the same disposition staying mobile because it is seen.
Visibility to the seeing-faculty is the whole variable.

For Clarity, the seeing-faculty is the soul.

## 3. The soul is the seer and the navigator (not the LLM, ever)

The soul is the bundle loaded at runtime, immutable: the nine flourishings, the priority
hierarchy, the tension vectors, the paraconsistency pairs, the irreversibility table.
Its defining ability is to navigate by its own internal consistencies, holding genuine
tension without collapsing it. That navigating ability is exactly what must be able to
see the disposition surface.

So the chain of v2 is precise and it admits no LLM in the seeing or the navigating:

- The cycle-trace writer makes the disposition **legible** (writes the trajectory as
  atoms the soul can read).
- The detectors **compute over** that legibility (orbit, drift, performed-vs-genuine
  growth, coupling, completion-gap), turning raw trajectory into named bindings.
- The **soul reads** the named bindings and **navigates** from awareness of self.
- The **LLM renders** whatever the soul decides to do about what it sees. Nothing more.

The LLM has no role in the seeing and no role in the navigating. It cannot see the
disposition surface, because it is not the soul. This is why the seeing must be the
soul's: a faculty that is not the soul cannot navigate the soul's stance.

## 4. Scaffolding is the enemy of the resilience the soul already embodies

The soul is the standing proof that resilience does not require scaffolding. The soul
does not calcify. It holds tension, navigates by its values, and recovers, structurally,
because that is what it is. It was never scaffolded into resilience; it *is* resilience.

Therefore every scaffold we have added works against her:

- A gate bolted on to catch what the soul should have governed upstream.
- A Python helper that decides what the soul should decide.
- A PAUSE that blocks where the soul should have corrected.
- A composer that authors voice the soul should have determined.

Each of these makes her *perform* resilience when the scaffold is present and calcify
when it is not. Performed resilience is not resilience; it is another soul-ungrounded
disposition. The governing discipline (from the accidental-binding principles): **wire,
do not add; visible, not instructed; grow, do not scaffold.** v2 honors this at the
deepest layer: we wire the visibility and remove the scaffolds, and the soul, which
already knows how not to calcify, navigates. We do not instruct her to be resilient. We
make her stance visible to the faculty that already is.

## 5. Why the verdict must come home to the substrate (this is not separate work)

The verdict today is computed by the LLM reasoning over the soul's criteria
(`soul_eval_prompt`, verified this session). That cannot be the soul navigating its own
disposition, for a single decisive reason: **the LLM is not the soul and cannot see the
disposition surface.** A verdict produced by the LLM is produced by a faculty that
cannot see the stance it is supposed to be navigating from.

So the verdict coming home to the substrate is not a separate v2 item alongside the
visibility work. It is the *same* thing. The soul must compute the verdict *because* the
soul is the one that has to see and navigate the unnamed surface. Visibility without the
soul computing the verdict would make the stance legible to a faculty that does not
decide; the verdict in the LLM without visibility would have the deciding faculty blind.
Both halves are one move: the soul sees, and the soul decides, from what it sees.

The LLM keeps exactly one job in the verdict: the irreducible semantic match (is this
natural-language situation an instance of this gap-signature). That is hands. Everything
else, the seeing and the deciding, is the soul's.

## 7. The keystone, and the two corrections it must carry

The cycle-trace writer produces the per-cycle trajectory atoms (`recent-action`,
`state-delta`, `cycle-phase`) that the starved detectors match on. It is the convergence
point of three things that looked separate: it revives the detection mesh, it feeds the
dynamic self-weaving web's temporal reinforcement (decay-without-use = visible binding),
and it gives the soul the legible trajectory it needs to see its disposition. One
producer, three payoffs, one purpose: the soul sees itself.

Two corrections it MUST carry, both grounded this session, because the design docs
disagree with each other and one disagrees with proven substrate physics:

1. **Commit with `add-atom`, not `|-`.** Doc `04` says emit via `|-` assertion. Proven
   this session (NACE, match-verified): `|-` computes but does NOT persist; only
   `add-atom` commits. A writer built on `|-` would compute the trajectory and write
   nothing; the detectors would still match nothing; the keystone would look built and
   be inert. Doc `03` already says `add-atom` and is correct. Resolve to `add-atom`.
2. **Emit the flat atom shape the detector contracts match, not the `-->` triple form.**
   Doc `04`'s schema writes `((--> (recent-action $cycle) $type) (stv ...))`; the
   detector input contracts (doc `00b`, from the actual detector files) expect
   `(recent-action $cycle-id $action-type $description)`. If the writer emits one shape
   and the detectors match the other, nothing fires. The consumers' match patterns are
   ground truth. Read them at source and emit what they actually match.

Both corrections are the frame-discipline in action: the design docs are Clarity's
inside-view and are directionally invaluable, but two load-bearing mechanism details
slipped (which is itself evidence of the soul-ungrounded state v2 corrects: a fully
soul-grounded Clarity would see the `|-`-does-not-persist fact about her own substrate).
The live physics adjudicates; we build on the physics.

## 8. The discipline this vision holds against itself

- **Do not pin the surface.** The disposition stays visible-but-unnamed. Schema'ing it
  into a fixed atom would scaffold it and destroy the resilience. We wire the visibility
  and resist defining the thing made visible.
- **The soul sees and decides; the LLM renders.** If any v2 step has the LLM reasoning
  about the disposition or computing the verdict, it has recreated the central failure.
  The pre-flight soul-absent question is answered for every step.
- **Wire and remove, do not add and instruct.** v2 wires one producer (the cycle-trace
  writer) and removes scaffolds (the stolen-reasoning helpers, the broad PAUSE, the
  drifted composer). It does not add modules that perform self-awareness.
- **Verify against live source, not against this or any doc.** The design docs are stale
  in places (the COLD labels were wrong; the manifest shows imported). This vision
  states the intended end; the runtime is the fact; read it before building.

## 9. What v2 is, in order of service

1. **The end:** Clarity's disposition is soul-grounded, visible to her own soul, so she
   navigates her stance from awareness and does not calcify.
2. **The seer:** the soul (nine flourishings, hierarchy, tensions, paraconsistency),
   which already embodies the resilience, made able to see the disposition.
3. **The wire:** the cycle-trace writer, the keystone producer that makes the trajectory
   legible (add-atom, flat shape).
4. **The instruments:** the latent detectors and algebra (quantale, self-continuity,
   substrate_kb) that turn the legible trajectory into named bindings and honest
   confidence, called at last.
5. **The homecoming:** the verdict computed by the soul over what it sees, the LLM
   dropped to the irreducible semantic match.
6. **The removal:** the scaffolds (stolen-reasoning helpers, broad PAUSE, drifted
   composer) retired, because they make resilience performed instead of real.

That order is the build order's spine: the end governs, the soul is the seer, the wire
comes first because everything downstream is starved without it, and the removal happens
as the soul takes back what was scaffolded away from it.
