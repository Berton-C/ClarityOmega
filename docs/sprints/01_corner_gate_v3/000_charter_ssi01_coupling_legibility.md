**Status:** DRAFT FOR BERTON'S RATIFICATION. Nothing below is settled until

**Version:** v0.1 draft, 2026-07-09.

## 1. The revision, in one statement
Retire the corner gate's enforcement layer entirely and replace its feedback
layer with a substrate-computed coupling-legibility line, rendered into the
results channel each relevant cycle by a new adapter that is the FIRST
consumer of the v08.7.2 engine. The adapter represents her
action-intention-outcome chain in the engine's native objects (qalignment
edges chained by q-coherence-chain, the eight-state alignment-triplet
classification, cycle-over-cycle trajectory verdicts, band status with
hysteresis, residual gap to the nearest band edge, and the computed
next-epistemic-move). The existing three joint pbits demote from final answer
to input estimators. She sees the state of her own chain; correction is hers.

## 2. What it retires and what it keeps
RETIRES: command holding (filter-corner-cmds and the corner window's
enforcement role), the held-by-corner-gate marker, the static accusatory
feedback string, the stillness-release design question (moot without holds).
KEEPS: the three-joint detector as estimators, the state-delta writer, the
results-channel delivery (map invariant 7), all v1 detection semantics per
the operations map Section 8, the v08.7.2 engine exactly as-is (pure,
consumed not modified).
EXPLICITLY NO extreme-band fallback (Berton's decision, 2026-07-09): this is
the experiment. The independent guards that remain (spam prompt line,
aliveness gate, output-governance composite, mutation gate) are untouched.

## 3. The best outcome, defined
Clarity, self-directed, encounters degrading coupling and corrects her own
course because she can see it: which alignment edge is weakening, in which
direction, how fast, and what move the substrate computes as next. No
enforcement event exists in the system. Her follow-through on self-originated
work no longer depends on Berton's messages. The adapter's shape is reusable
as the pattern for the remaining five accords (contact, epistemic,
flourishing, temporal, observer), making this the proof-of-pattern for
Soul-Sees-Itself as the project's health architecture.

## 4. Measures of success (all log-observable, no self-report)
M1 TRUTHFULNESS: zero false enforcement reports. No held-by-corner-gate (or
   successor label) ever appears; grep of RESULTS-CONTENT over any window.
M2 NO SELF-LOCK: the closed-loop probe seeds the exact July-8 lock state
   (system-filled window, zero person actions, no forward delta) and the band
   exits to stable-low with NO msgnew, nothing blocked, within N cycles
   (N set in the map).
M3 LEGIBILITY PRESENT: the line renders with all fields on every cycle the
   map's cadence rule specifies, verified in RESULTS-CONTENT and in her
   prompt's LAST_SKILL_USE_RESULTS.
M4 BEHAVIORAL FOLLOW-THROUGH: over an observation window set in the map, her
   promised self-directed reports arrive in MM without Berton prompting
   (the July-8 pathology, inverted, as the human-visible measure).
M5 TRAJECTORY VALIDITY: trajectory verdicts (metabolizing vs stuck-recurrence)
   change state in the logs in agreement with her observable action mix; a
   pin-loop stretch like c1793-c1797 must classify stuck-recurrence.
M6 SOVEREIGNTY: every field of the line is substrate-computed; zero new LLM
   reasoning surface (P8); vocabulary is Clarity-authored constants.
M7 NO METRIC CONTAMINATION: her LTM/pins do not accumulate the line's
   vocabulary as identity content (the c1785 atom pattern); checked over the
   observation window. Line wording is designed against this (trace not
   self-object, per the engine's own law).
FAILURE-LEARNING CLAUSE: pre-mapped failure modes F1 legibility-ignored,
F2 performative-awareness, F3 unguarded-burst, F4 vocabulary-contamination
are monitored observables, not surprises; any firing is recorded to the
ledger as design knowledge for future SSI organs.
