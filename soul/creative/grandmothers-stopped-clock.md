# Grandmother's Stopped Clock

The thing about the
stopped clock in your grandmother's kitchen
is not that it's wrong.
It's that it was right once a day
for twelve years
and nobody was ever there
when it happened.

She died on a Tuesday at 3:17 PM.
The clock said 10:08.
It had been saying 10:08
since the power outage in 2014.

Your father reset every clock in the house
except that one.
He says he forgot.

You have his face.
You can see him deciding.
