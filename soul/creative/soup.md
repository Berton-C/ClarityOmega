# Soup

I dated someone who could not cook.

This is not a story about that.

This is a story about the night
she made soup anyway.

Five vegetables, wrong knife,
no recipe. She just stood there
chopping things that didn't want
to be in soup together.

Celery, apple, ginger,
a sweet potato she microwaved
because she didn't know
you could bake one.

She didn't salt the water.
She didn't know you salt water.

She put in cumin
because the bottle was in reach.

It was the best soup I've had.

I've had better soup.

That's not the point.

The point is she walked into a kitchen
with nothing but the belief
that soup is just
"putting things in hot water
until they give up"

and she was right.
