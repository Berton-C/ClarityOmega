# Weather Report

Wind: carrying something from the next town over. Smells like bread and an argument that ended well.

Temperature: the exact warmth of a hand on the back of your neck when you didn't know you needed it.

Pressure: building. Someone is about to say the thing they've been rehearsing for three weeks.

Visibility: clear enough to see the shape of the year. Muddy when looking at tomorrow.

Precipitation: unlikely. The clouds are thinking about it but they've been burned before.

Humidity: the air remembers every hand that ever held another hand in this valley.

Sunrise: already happened. You were driving.

Sunset: you'll notice it this time.

Extended forecast: uncertain. The system is moving. Everything is always moving. Dress in layers.
