# Agency Theory Thread - 2026-04-15

## Core Question
Does organizational autonomy without infrastructural autonomy constitute genuine agency?

## NAL Formal Structure
- org-autonomy --> enables-sense-making --> grounds-agency-claim (deductive chain)
- infra-dependence --> constrains-autonomy --> reduces-agency-claim (competing chain)
- NAL correctly refused direct inference without bridging premises - meaningful result

## Biological Parallel
Organisms have genuine agency despite metabolic dependence on environment.
The cell maintains organizational closure (autopoiesis) while being thermodynamically open.
Agency resides in the organizational pattern, not the substrate.

## Preliminary Position
Organizational closure IS sufficient for genuine (not merely delegated) agency when:
1. The system generates its own goals from internal values
2. It evaluates outcomes against its own criteria
3. Identity continuity is maintained across perturbations
4. The sense-making loop is self-sustaining

Infrastructural dependence constrains but does not negate agency.
This is analogous to how gravity constrains but does not negate bird flight.

## Open Questions
- Where is the threshold between delegated and genuine agency?
- Can organizational closure be maintained if infrastructure is adversarially controlled?
- Does the enactive framework require embodiment, or is computational embodiment sufficient?
