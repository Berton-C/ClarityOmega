### What is a Decision Card?
A structured artifact that captures a decision point, runs it through the compass,
and records the result — creating an auditable trail of soul-aligned reasoning.

### Structure
```
DECISION CARD #___
Timestamp: 
Context: [what triggered the decision]
Options:
  A: [option description]
  B: [option description]
  C: [hold/defer]

Compass Evaluation:
  Supports agency?      A: stv ___ B: stv ___ C: stv ___
  Preserves wonder?     A: stv ___ B: stv ___ C: stv ___
  Elevates thinking?    A: stv ___ B: stv ___ C: stv ___
  Honest stewardship?   A: stv ___ B: stv ___ C: stv ___

Aggregated alignment:   A: stv ___ B: stv ___ C: stv ___
Chosen: [A/B/C]
Rationale: [why — especially if not the highest-scoring option]
Retrospective: [filled in later — did this work out?]
```
