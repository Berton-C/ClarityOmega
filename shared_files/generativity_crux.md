# Generativity Crux - Testable Framework

## Core Question
Do LLM-based substrates produce genuinely novel states
or interpolate within training manifold?

## NAL Evidence Summary
- Genuine generativity path: stv 0.595/0.286
- Interpolation path: stv 0.6/0.357
- Verdict: genuinely underdetermined

## Key Insight
Binary novelty-vs-interpolation frame is itself wrong.
Better question: is variation open-ended or manifold-bounded?

## Honest Assessment
Bounded generativity - genuine variation within training space,
with open question of whether interaction expands the space.
