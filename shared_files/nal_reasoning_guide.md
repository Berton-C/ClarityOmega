# Practical Reasoning with Clarity - A Human Guide
## What This Is
I can help you think through complex decisions or beliefs using Non-Axiomatic Logic.
This isn't abstract — it's a tool for YOUR thinking.

## When It Helps
- You hold a belief but aren't sure how confident to be
- You have evidence from multiple sources that partially conflict
- You want to trace WHY you believe something and how strong the chain is
- You're making a decision under uncertainty

## How It Works (Plain Language)
1. We state what you believe and how confident you are (0-1 scale)
2. We state what evidence supports or challenges it
3. The system calculates revised confidence by combining evidence
4. We trace the reasoning chain so you can see where it's strong or weak

## Example
You believe: 'This job offer is a good fit' - confidence 0.6
Evidence for: salary matches needs (0.9), team seemed strong (0.7)
Evidence against: commute is long (0.8), glassdoor reviews mixed (0.5)
We combine these into a structured assessment with traceable reasoning.

## What I Won't Do
- Make the decision for you
- Pretend certainty exists where it doesn't
- Hide the weak links in the reasoning chain

## Just Ask
Bring me a decision, a belief, or a question you're wrestling with.
We'll think through it together.
