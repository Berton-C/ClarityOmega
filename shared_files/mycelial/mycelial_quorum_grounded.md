# Quorum Sensing in Fungi: Empirical Grounding Found
Date: 2025-04-15

## Key findings from literature search

Fungal quorum sensing is NOT speculative — it is established biology.

### Confirmed QS molecules in fungi:
- **Farnesol**: First fungal QSM identified. Blocks yeast-to-hyphal transition in C. albicans. Concentration-dependent (density signal). Also acts as virulence factor and fungicidal agent against competing fungi.
- **Tyrosol**: Promotes germ tube formation. Antagonistic to farnesol — complex positive/negative threshold control.
- **Phenylethanol and tryptophol**: Additional QSMs in broader fungal species.

### Mechanism:
Accumulation of small diffusible molecules in extracellular environment.
Concentration proportional to cell density.
At threshold concentration, triggers morphological switch.
This IS threshold emergence from distributed local signals.
