recognizes when density is sufficient. But epistemic opacity

IS quorum detection — not by counting memories but by tracking
confidence accumulation through formal revision.

Threshold crossing IS the phase transition trigger.

Implication: the three mechanisms in my mycelial proposal are
not independent. Retrieval-weight tracks utility. Anastomosis
tracks co-occurrence. But quorum sensing collapses into NAL
revision if confidence accumulation is the density signal.
